# CAMEO Demo Prompts (80/20)

Use any of these with your own image, or use scenario presets in the UI.

## Distress
1. I feel very low and alone today.
2. Everything feels heavy and I need support.
3. I am exhausted and overwhelmed right now.

## Celebration
1. I got selected and I am so happy.
2. Today is amazing and I feel proud of myself.
3. I achieved my goal and I am excited.

## Frustration
1. I am frustrated because nothing works.
2. I keep getting blocked and I feel annoyed.
3. This keeps failing despite all my effort.

## Neutral
1. Today was normal and routine.
2. Nothing special, just a regular day.
3. Things are steady and mostly fine.

## Seeking Help
1. I feel anxious and I need guidance on what to do next.
2. I am worried and could use practical support.
3. I need help figuring this out step by step.

## Gratitude
1. Thank you for supporting me, I feel hopeful now.
2. I really appreciate this help and I am grateful.
3. This support meant a lot to me and I feel positive.
